[ HOW IT WORKS ]

Think of Git Agent like a super-smart digital librarian for code. Instead of you having to search through millions of messy files to find what you need, you just tell the Agent what you want in normal, everyday words.

Step 1: Understand. You type "I want a game engine." The Agent translates your English into complex search code.
Step 2: Search. It sprints into the massive GitHub library and grabs the best matches.
Step 3: Read. It literally opens the "instruction manuals" (READMEs) of those projects and speed-reads them to make sure they are actually good.
Step 4: Deliver. It brings the top 3 best projects right to your screen.
